Our Vision

We believe the internet needs less noise and more understanding. Clarion is our answer—a tool designed not for arguments, but for insight. By focusing on consensus and commonality, we aim to build a more thoughtful and constructive space for global conversation.

The Technology: The Clarity Engine

1. Vectorization

Every answer is converted into a numerical "vector" that represents its semantic meaning, not just its keywords.

2. Clustering

Our AI uses advanced algorithms to group answers with similar meanings into thematic clusters.

3. Summarization

Finally, the engine generates a new, clean summary that represents the core idea of each cluster.
